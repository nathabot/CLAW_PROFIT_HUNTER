## Growth Tactics

### 1. Engagement Loop (Daily)

```
1. Follow 20-30 relevant accounts
   - Search: #Solana, #DeFi, #Crypto, #AI
   - Target: People who post about trading
   
2. Like their posts (30-50/day)
   - Quality likes, not spam
   - Add thoughtful likes
   
3. Reply to interesting posts
   - Add value, not just "nice!"
   - Ask questions
   
4. Some will follow back
   - Natural growth
```
