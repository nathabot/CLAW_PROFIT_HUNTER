## 1. SCALPING STRATEGY

### What is Scalping?
- Trading strategy for **minor price changes** in brief periods
- **10 to few hundred trades per day**
- Small profits compound into large gains
- **Strict exit strategy REQUIRED** to prevent large losses

### Key Principles:
1. **Larger position sizes** for smaller price gains
2. **Shortest holding time** possible
3. **Technical analysis** focused
4. **Fast execution** essential
5. **Disciplined management** crucial

### My Mistakes:
- Chased large moves (+46%) instead of minor changes
- No strict exit strategy (couldn't even sell!)
- Wrong position sizing (0.02 SOL too small after fees)

### Correct Approach:
- Many small profits (2-5% per trade)
- Strict stop loss EVERY trade
- Fast in, fast out
- Risk management first

## 2. RISK MANAGEMENT

### The #1 Rule:
> "Risk management is one of the most important topics in trading. Many traders overlook this and just gamble."

### Key Concepts:
1. **Protect capital first**, profits second
2. **Long-term ROI** matters, not jackpot
3. **Casinos make money** because they manage risk, gamblers don't

### Risk Management Rules:
- Never risk more than **1-2% per trade**
- Always use **stop losses**
- Position size based on **account size**, not greed
- **Cut losses quickly**, let winners run

- Risked ~25% of account in 2 trades (0.02/0.08 = 25%)
- No stop loss mechanism (couldn't sell!)
- Gambled for jackpot instead of steady profits

- Risk max 1% per trade = 0.0008 SOL max loss
- Stop loss automatic, not optional
- Position size: 0.01 SOL max per trade

## 4. LESSONS FROM $290 LOSS

### What NOT to Do:
1. Chase pumps (buying at +46%)
2. No exit plan before entry
3. Risk too much per trade
4. Build complex systems without testing
5. Skip paper trading
6. No risk/reward calculation

### What TO Do:
1. Define entry, exit, stop loss BEFORE trading
2. Risk max 1-2% per trade
3. Paper trade 10+ times first
4. Simple strategy, perfect execution
5. Cut losses at -5% (not -38%!)
6. Take profits at 2:1 reward/risk
