# Pump.fun Launch Strategy - Success Guide

## How Pump.fun Works

### Token Lifecycle:
1. **Create** → Token deployed with 0% supply to creator
2. **Bonding Curve** → Token trades in curve, price increases with buys
3. **Graduation** → When MC reaches $12k, migrates to Raydium
4. **Fail** → If MC drops to $0, token dies

### Key Metrics:
- **Success Rate:** ~10-15% tokens graduate to Raydium
- **Average Time:** 2-4 hours to $12k (for successful ones)
- **Volume needed:** ~$50-100k total volume for graduation

## Success Factors (What Makes Tokens Succeed)

### 1. Twitter/X Presence (CRITICAL)
- Need 1000+ followers BEFORE launch
- Pre-announce with hype
- Post consistently during launch
- Influencer retweets

### 2. Narrative/Meme
- Simple, catchy story
- Relatable to crypto Twitter
- Easy to understand in 1 sentence

### 3. Early Momentum
- First 10-20 buyers get bonus
- Create FOMO
- Keep buy pressure for first hour

### 4. Trending on Pump.fun
- Get to "Trending" section = free visibility
- Needs volume in first 30 mins

### 5. Community
- Telegram group ready
- Discord ready
- Fans ready to buy
